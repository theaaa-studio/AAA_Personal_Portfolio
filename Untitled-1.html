About

I started my computer science journey as a problem solver, traveling across the country for programming contests—something I still love doing today. Solving problems and pushing my skills has always been my thing.

Game development keeps me hooked. Even after 8 years, I'm still learning, creating, and playing. Lately, XR technology has grabbed my interest, and I get pumped whenever I build something new in this space.

I just love building stuff. Over the years, I've made games, apps, websites, and desktop applications—each one teaching me something new.

I also had the chance to mentor a school programmer who competed at the National Girls Programming Contest 2019, which was a proud moment for me.

Programming is my strong suit. I believe logic matters more than the language, so I adapt fast and code across different technologies with ease.

With 7 years in game dev and 5 in XR, I've designed and built games, XR apps, Web3D experiences, interactive architectural tools, and utilities. My experience has sharpened my skills and fueled my creativity.

For the past 2 years, I've been the Lead Developer at my organization, handling everything from planning and design to development and deployment. I work closely with clients, project managers, and my team to ensure we deliver top-notch results. Turning ideas into reality and leading a team to create something amazing is what keeps me going.

At the end of the day, whether it's coding, mentoring, or exploring new tech, it's all about building, learning, and pushing boundaries.
